Budget Passbook

Privacy Policy

How your data is handled

Short version

Your budget data is stored only on your device. When you use one of the app's built-in AI features (category suggestions, spending insights, or chat), the relevant data is sent to a small relay we operate, which forwards it to Anthropic (our AI provider) to generate a response, and is not stored anywhere afterward.

What data is stored, and where

The app stores the following information, entirely on your device:

  • Your monthly income figure
  • Your budget category names and amounts
  • Your expense and income ledger entries (description, category, amount, date)
  • A randomly generated device identifier, used only to apply a fair daily limit on AI usage

This data lives in your browser's local storage on your phone. It is not uploaded, backed up, or synced to any cloud service, and we have no access to it beyond what's described below for AI features.

How the AI features work

Budget Passbook includes built-in AI features — category suggestions, spending insights, and a chat assistant — powered by Anthropic's Claude models. Unlike a "bring your own key" setup, these work automatically for every user, without you needing to sign up for anything.

When you use an AI feature, the relevant data (such as an expense description, or a short summary of your budget and recent entries) is sent from your device to a relay server we operate, which adds our API credentials and forwards the request to Anthropic. The response is passed straight back to your device. We do not log, store, or retain the content of these requests — the relay only keeps a daily count per device (via the anonymous device identifier) so we can apply a fair usage limit.

Anthropic's handling of data sent to its API is governed by its own privacy policy, available at anthropic.com/legal/privacy.

What we don't do

  • No account creation, login, or personal identifiers are required
  • No analytics, tracking, or advertising SDKs are included
  • No data is shared with, or sold to, any third party other than Anthropic, and only the minimum needed to answer your AI request
  • No internet connection is required for the app's core budgeting features once installed — only the AI features need connectivity

Clearing your data

Uninstalling the app, or clearing site data/storage for it from your browser settings, permanently deletes all information the app has stored. There is no recovery once cleared, since no copy exists anywhere else.

Changes to this policy

If this policy is ever updated, the revised version will be posted at this same page.
